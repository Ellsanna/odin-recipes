Spaghetti

A great meal that only takes a few minutes

Spaghetti is always a family favorite, and only a few ingredients are need to make this fulfilling meal

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or any other meat (if desired)

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil
  2. Boil spaghtetti noodles in water until the noodles are soft, but not mushy
  3. Once noodles are done, drain water from them
  4. Brown beef or other meat in a skillet and drain grease
  5. Warm up tomato sauce in another pot
  6. Combine meat and sauce if applicable
  7. Combine noodles and sauce
